- :material-text: **Run description:** PM_IBI_run1 description: For each topic, we automatically expand the terms describing the related disease(s), gene(s) and variant(s) by relying on the UMLS meta-thesaurus and semantic network and on dbSNP. In particular for each gene and disease term we automatically gathered from UMLS: (i) all its synonym terms; (ii) all the terms describing more specific concepts (hyponyms) by means of the UMLS semantic network (traversed up to a maximum depth of three levels). When variant(s) are present, we retrieved its reference SNP ID number (when possible).
